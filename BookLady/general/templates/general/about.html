What we are aiming for

Welcome to The BookLady, where we're dedicated to nurturing a lifelong love for learning among young minds through an engaging and competitive web application.

Why we are doing this:

  • We believe quality education should be accessible to all (aligned with SDG4), and want to make a fun experience for everyone to enjoy.

What you can do:

  • Create a personalized account: Create your own profile to track your reading progress.
  • Compete with fellow users and friends based on the pages and books you've read.
  • Earn milestone badges as you read.
  • Add friends, compare points, and engage in friendly competition.
  • Compete with your friends to maintain reading streaks.
  • Search for books, and leave reviews to help others.
  • Explore bestsellers recommended by The New York Times on our Home page.
